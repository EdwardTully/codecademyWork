Established in 1932 by Cletus Clefton, Bayview Harbor Sailing Association has served the Bayview area in a vast number of endevours. We are a open type of club with a diverse membership. We seek to provide a community of fellowship, scholarship, adventure, challenges, sportsmanship, and fun for everyone and all ages. We also want to be an inclusive part of the community and offer several membership packages to allow most anyone to become a part of our association. Furthermore, the association has a strong program offering in person courses in sailing and seamanship, open to the public and free to members. Our clubhouse, outdoor pool, tiki bar, restaurant, locker room, and spa will reward you after a long day or a long week. Weekend races will exhilarate your senses of competition and victory. Club trips will rejuvinate your senses of adventure. And club parties will help you connect with a mistress.
-Commodore Perkins
